## KPI для инвестиционных компаний (недвижимость)

### 8 типов RE-дашбордов (GoodData)
Источник: https://www.gooddata.com/blog/real-estate-dashboard-examples-that-drive-smarter-decisions/

1. **Portfolio Dashboard**: Total value, ROI, Cap rate, Occupancy, Maintenance costs
2. **Investment Dashboard**: Rental income, Cost per unit, NOI, Holding period
3. **Sales Dashboard**: Leads, Conversion, Closing time, Sale price, Agent performance
4. **Financial Dashboard**: NOI, Gross income, Management costs, Debt load

### Ключевые инвестиционные метрики
- **IRR** (Internal Rate of Return) — учитывает time value of money
- **Cap Rate** — rental income / property value
- **Cash-on-Cash Return** — annual cash flow / total cash invested
- **NOI** (Net Operating Income)
- **Equity Multiple** — total distributions / total equity invested

### Профессиональный софт для RE dashboards
